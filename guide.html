Optimal Strategy

Phase 1 — Early Life (no rebirth)

  1. Stay on Beggar (0 expenses) while grinding Concentration first — it multiplies all skill XP, compounding every other skill.
  2. Grind Bargaining simultaneously — slashes all expenses toward a 10% floor. The single biggest balance protector.
  3. Buy Book (10 coins/day) as soon as Fisherman income covers it — ×1.5 all skill XP for very low cost.
  4. Upgrade to Farmer → Fisherman → Miner only after Bargaining is high enough that income comfortably exceeds expenses.

Phase 2 — Mid Life

  1. Level Productivity — ×2.0 job XP at max, lets you reach higher-paying jobs much faster.
  2. Level Meditation — happiness multiplies all XP. Stacks with everything.
  3. Upgrade housing only when net income ≥ 2× housing expense as a safety buffer.
  4. Unlock Military or Arcane track based on your rebirth count and available skills.

Phase 3 — Late Life / Rebirth Prep

  1. Level Immortality (and later Super immortality) — more time = more everything.
  2. Level Time warping — increases raw game speed. Most powerful late-game skill.
  3. Aim for Mage / Wizard on the Arcane track for best income per XP invested.
  4. Rebirth once Merchant/Knight/Mage is comfortably reached to carry max-level bonuses forward.
⚠️ Auto-promote warning: Squire pays only 5 coins/day — worse than Beggar. Switching to the Military track mid-life with auto-promote enabled can instantly bankrupt you. Disable auto-promote before switching tracks intentionally.
