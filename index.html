How to Play

You play as a character caught in a cycle of reincarnation. Each life has a fixed lifespan — use it to earn coins through jobs, stack multipliers through skills, and push as far as you can before time runs out. When you rebirth, key achievements carry forward into your next life.

  • Jobs earn coins every day. Click a job row to switch to it.
  • Skills grant passive multipliers (XP speed, income, lifespan…). Click a skill row to train it.
  • Shop lets you buy housing and equipment that further boost stats — but they cost coins per day.
  • Auto-promote automatically switches you to the next job when requirements are met. Caution: can cause bankruptcy if you enter Military track.
  • Auto-learn automatically trains the skill with the lowest level (skipping skills marked Skip).
  • When you reach age 25, the Amulet tab unlocks — touch the eye to rebirth and carry max-level bonuses into your next life.
  • Story events trigger at milestone levels and may grant temporary bonuses.

你扮演一个在轮回中不断重生的角色。每一世都有固定的寿命上限，你需要在有限的时间内通过职业赚取金币、通过技能积累倍率加成，并在寿命耗尽前尽可能走得更远。转生后，部分成就将延续到下一世。

  • 职业每天产生金币收入。点击职业行可切换职业。
  • 技能提供被动倍率（经验速度、收入、寿命等）。点击技能行可开始训练。
  • 商店可购买住房和装备，进一步提升属性——但每天需要支出金币。
  • 自动晋升会在满足条件时自动切换到下一个职业。注意：进入军事路线时可能导致破产。
  • 自动学习会自动训练等级最低的技能（跳过勾选了"跳过"的技能）。
  • 当你达到 25岁时，护符栏解锁——触摸眼睛即可转生，将最高等级加成带入下一世。
  • 故事事件在关键等级触发，可能带来临时加成。

Optimal Strategy

Phase 1 — Early Life

  1. Stay on Beggar (0 expenses) and grind Concentration first — it multiplies all skill XP.
  2. Grind Bargaining simultaneously — slashes all expenses toward a 10% floor.
  3. Buy Book (10 coins/day) as soon as Fisherman income covers it — ×1.5 all skill XP.
  4. Upgrade Farmer → Fisherman → Miner only after Bargaining is high and income exceeds expenses.

Phase 2 — Mid Life

  1. Level Productivity — ×2.0 job XP at max, reach high-paying jobs much faster.
  2. Level Meditation — happiness multiplies all XP, stacks with everything.
  3. Upgrade housing only when net income ≥ 2× housing expense.
  4. Unlock Military or Arcane track based on your rebirth count.

Phase 3 — Late Life

  1. Level Immortality + Super immortality — more time = more everything.
  2. Level Time warping — multiplies raw game speed. Most powerful late-game skill.
  3. Aim for Mage / Wizard on Arcane for best income per XP invested.
⚠️ Auto-promote warning: Squire pays only 5 coins/day — worse than Beggar. Disable auto-promote before switching to Military track.

第一阶段 — 生命初期

  1. 留在乞丐（零支出），优先刷专注——倍增所有技能经验。
  2. 同步刷议价——将支出压至10%下限，是保持余额的最重要手段。
  3. 渔夫收入能覆盖后，购买书本（10枚/天）——以极低代价获得×1.5技能经验。
  4. 只有在议价足够高、收入稳定后，再升职 农民→渔夫→矿工。

第二阶段 — 生命中期

  1. 刷效率——满级×2.0职业经验，大幅加速升至高收入职业。
  2. 刷冥想——幸福度倍增所有经验，与其他加成叠乘。
  3. 升级住房前确保 净收入 ≥ 新支出的2倍。
  4. 根据转生次数选择进入军事或秘法协会路线。

第三阶段 — 生命末期

  1. 刷不朽＋超级不朽——时间越长，收益越高。
  2. 刷时间扭曲——直接提升游戏速度，后期最强技能。
  3. 秘法路线以法师/巫师为目标，单位经验的收入回报最高。
⚠️ 自动晋升警告：侍从每天仅赚5枚——比乞丐还差。切换军事路线前请先关闭自动晋升。
